Pattern problems are based on some key rules:

- Row
- Columns
- Row means the outer loop
- Column means the inner loop

- Key points:

    1. outer loops are denoted by i most of the time
    2. inner loops are denoted by j most of the time
    3. outer and inner loops are used to occupy the places.

- range() function is used mainly to loop through rows and Columns.

- About Range function:

    1. range function is mainly used to loop through a specific range
    2. Example:

            - range(1, 6) = it will start from 1 and stop at 5
            - formula = (start -> stop-1)
            - like the example , it starts from 1 and ends at 5 (1 --- 5 (6-1))
            - It iterates one by one like, 

                    - 1, 2, 3, 4, 5
                    - If there is any specific task in each iterations, it will complete that's specific iteration's 
                      work then will move to the next iteration.
